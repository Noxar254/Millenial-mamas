Our Story

From Silent Struggles to a Sisterhood of Strength

Millennial Mamas was born out of a quiet pain—one that too many mothers carry alone.

Founded in August 2022 by Salome Oyosi Faith and co-founded by Serah Seroney, our journey began with a simple but powerful truth:

no woman should have to walk the path of motherhood alone. Especially not the teenage mom overwhelmed by stigma,

the widow trying to stay strong for her children, the divorced mom finding her voice again,

or the married woman navigating motherhood without support in her own home... learn more

Fuel the Mission

Stand with us in building strong, self-reliant mothers and communities.

Your support is not charity — it's an investment in transformation. We equip single mothers with tools to heal, grow, and thrive. Together, we create a ripple effect that empowers generations.

Why Your Support Matters

Many single mothers face emotional trauma, economic hardship, and social exclusion. Through wellness programs, mentorship, shelter, and education, Millennial Mamas helps them rebuild their confidence and take charge of their future.

Your contribution powers therapy sessions, food support, training programs, and community outreach. It's how we turn breakdowns into breakthroughs and ensure no woman walks alone.

Our Founders & Leadership

Meet the visionary women whose passion, pain, and purpose birthed this sisterhood

Founder

Salome Oyosi Faith

Founder & Visionary Leader

An Advocate of the High Court of Kenya with over a decade of legal experience, Salome combines her professional expertise with lived experience as a single mother to build safe, healing spaces for moms. From battling stigma to pioneering a movement reaching 400+ women, she's redefining advocacy both inside and outside the courtroom.

Advocate Mother Visionary
Co-Founder

Serah Seroney

Co-Founder & Strategic Partner

With a heart for community building and strategic thinking, Serah has been instrumental in shaping Millennial Mamas into the thriving organization it is today. Her dedication to supporting mothers is evident in every program we launch.

Our Leadership Team

Behind Millennial Mamas is a passionate team of leaders, advocates, and change-makers who understand the unique challenges faced by single mothers. Our governance structure ensures accountability, transparency, and effective decision-making.

Executive Board of Directors

Nine dedicated leaders providing strategic guidance and governance oversight

Our Executive Board comprises nine experienced professionals and community leaders who bring diverse expertise in areas including healthcare, finance, education, social work, and nonprofit management. Together, they provide strategic direction, ensure fiscal responsibility, and uphold our mission to empower single mothers across Kenya and beyond.

Each board member is committed to our vision of creating supportive communities where single mothers can thrive. They volunteer their time, expertise, and resources to help us reach more mothers and create lasting impact in their lives.
