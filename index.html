Our Story

From Silent Struggles to a Sisterhood of Strength

Millennial Mamas was born out of a quiet pain—one that too many mothers carry alone.

Founded in August 2022 by Salome Oyosi Faith and co-founded by Serah Seroney, our journey began with a simple but powerful truth:

no woman should have to walk the path of motherhood alone. Especially not the teenage mom overwhelmed by stigma,

the widow trying to stay strong for her children, the divorced mom finding her voice again,

or the married woman navigating motherhood without support in her own home... learn more
